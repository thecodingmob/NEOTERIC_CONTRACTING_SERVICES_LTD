Who Are We?

We believe that quality service is the foundation to every successful project.

Neoteric Contracting Services Limited is a professional general contracting, facility management and maintanance company committed to delivering reliable, efficient and high-quality solutions for residential, commercial and industrial clients. We specialize in creating and maintaining functional, safe and sustainable environments through innovative service delivery and technical expertise. With a strong focus on excellence, professionalism and customer satisfaction, we provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of every client while maintaining the highest standards in the industry.

Our company is built on integrity, innovation, teamwork and technical competence. We combine modern approaches and industry best practices to deliver projects and maintain services that exceed expectations.

What We Do

Our facility management and maintenance services
are designed to ensure comfort, safety, efficiency and proper upkeep of residential properties.

Electrical Work

We ensure the safe and efficient distribution of power within a facility. This includes installation, maintenance, troubleshooting and repairs of electrical systems, lighting, wiring, panels, and power equipment to prevent hazards, reduce downtime and improve energy efficiency.

Repairs

We focus on fixing damaged, faulty, or worn-out parts of a building or equipment. Timely repairs help maintain the functionality, safety, and appearance of the facility while preventing minor issues from becoming costly problems.

Renovation

We are involved in upgrading, remodeling, or improving existing spaces and structures. It helps enhance the value, functionality, comfort and aesthetics of a property while adapting facilities to modern standards and changing needs.

Plumbing

We manage the installation, maintenance, and repair of water supply systems, pipes, fittings, and sanitary equipment. Proper plumbing ensures clean water flow, effective waste disposal and prevent leakages or water damage.

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning goes beyond regular cleaning by thoroughly sanitizing and disinfecting spaces, surfaces and hidden areas. It promotes hygiene, improves indoor air quality, creates a healthy environment, and maintains the professional appearance of the facility.

Mechanical Services

We involve the maintenance and operation of mechanical systems such as HVAC systems, generators, pumps, elevators and machinery.

Preventive Maintenance

We provide routine inspections and servicing of equipments and facility systems before faults occur.

General Facility Maintenance

We provide continuous upkeep of the property, including inspections, replacements, servicing and monitoring of facility assets.

Our Goal

Our objectives and goals.

At Neoteric Contracting Services Ltd, we are dedicated to building lasting relationships through trust, quality workmanship, and outstanding service delivery. Our goal is to provide solutions that improve the comfort, safety, efficiency and value of every property we manage or maintain.

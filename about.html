About Us

NEOTERIC CONTRACTING SERVICES LIMITED

Neoteric Contracting Services was established on the 6th of June, 2022 by Mr. ISAH S. RAYMOND with a vision to provide innovative, reliable, and professional contracting solutions across multiple industries. The company was officially incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2024. Despite being relatively young within the industry, Neoteric Contracting Services has successfully handled significant projects across different sectors and regions within Nigeria, demonstrating excellence, professionalism, and commitment to delivering quality services. Driven by innovation and client satisfaction, Neoteric Contracting Services continues to position itself as a trusted partner for comprehensive contracting solutions, offering services tailored to meet evolving industrial and commercial demands.

About The Founder

ISAH S. RAYMOND

He is a Nigerian professional with extensive experience spanning multiple industries and sectors within Nigeria and across other African countries. Having served in various professional capacities throughout his career, he possesses broad industry knowledge and practical expertise gained from years of working across different sectors and professions. He is a certified FinOps Practitioner and holds professional certification in Project Management from Stafford University. His contributions to various industries, combined with his professional experience and passion for delivering innovative solutions, inspired the establishment of Neoteric Contracting Services. Under his leadership, the company continues to pursue excellence while building a reputation founded on integrity, innovation, and quality service delivery.

Partenership Strategies

We believe that strong partnerships are essential for delivering quality projects and achieving long-term growth.

Our partnership strategy focuses on:

  1. Building strategic alliances with reputable construction, engineering, and facility management firms.
  2. Collaborating with government agencies, private organizations, and development partners on infrastructure and community development projects.
  3. Partnering with local suppliers and manufacturers to ensure timely delivery of quality materials and services.

  6. Creating mutually beneficial partnerships that foster innovation, efficiency, and sustainable development.
